rasterized-pdfs-poc

Takes a PDF, rasterizes it, and outputs a PDF again.
This is useful, e.g., for compressing PDFs with lots of vector handwriting, such as exported by Notability or GoodNotes.
All processing is done in the browser, the file never leaves your device.
